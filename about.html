About Pathway

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind your unforgettable travel experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate travelers, Pathway began with a simple mission: to create authentic travel experiences that connect people with the world's most incredible destinations.

What started as a small operation with just three employees has grown into a global travel company with offices in five countries and a network of local partners around the world. Despite our growth, we've remained true to our founding principles of authenticity, sustainability, and exceptional service.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of travelers discover new cultures, create lasting memories, and develop a deeper appreciation for our beautiful planet. We believe that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and foster global understanding.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To create transformative travel experiences that inspire, educate, and connect people with the world's diverse cultures and environments, while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve natural resources.

Authenticity

We create genuine experiences that go beyond tourist attractions to reveal the true essence of each destination.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible travel practices that respect and preserve local cultures and environments.

Community

We believe in supporting local communities through ethical partnerships and fair economic opportunities.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from planning to execution and follow-up.
